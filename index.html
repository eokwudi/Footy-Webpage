Cristiano Ronaldo - Juventus FWD

From the money, to the cars, to the clothes it can seem that fame comes naturally to Cristiano Ronaldo. He has spent years as the most popular footballer on the planet and has in turn garnered the admiration and disdain of many. Behind that chiseled exterior though is a simple boy from Madeira who spent his youth training relentlessly, even when no food was available. His hard work and determination has cemented his place in football history as one of the greatest and CR7 does not seem to be slowing any time soon

Virgil Van Dijk - Liverpool CB

When Liverpool signed Virgil Van Dijk from Southampton during the 2017/2018 season, no defender had ever had more pressure to be an immediate success but Van Dijk met and exceeded the pressure with aplomb. From winning the PFA's Player of the Year award to leading Liverpool to a Champions League title, Van Dijk has brought the defender position back to the forefront.
